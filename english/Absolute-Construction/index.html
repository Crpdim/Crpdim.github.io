# 表达 “一大群人”

# an army of sb / armies of sb

后面一般都跟做同一件事儿的人

an army of volunteers 大批志愿者 armies of shoppers 成群的购物者

# a swarm of sb / swarms of sb

多指移动的人群

Swarms of tourists jostled through the square. 一群游客推搡着穿过广场

# 涌满

The beaches were swarming with tourists. 沙滩上挤满了游客

# a throng of sb / throngs of sb

指一大群人在一个地方

# a stream of sb

指一连串，一系列

  • a stream of traffic 川流不息的车辆
  • a stream of abuse 一连串的辱骂
  • A steady stream of visitors came to the house. 游客络绎不绝地来参观这所房子。

# a horde of sb / hordes of sb

多用于指吵嚷的一群人

# 表示涌入

flock to + 地点

Hundreds of thousands of young people flock to cities, driven by a desire for a higher income and more job opportunities . 数不清的年轻人被高收入和更多工作机会的欲望驱使着涌入城市。

# 表示瞥见

catch a glimpse of 瞥见

To catch a glimpse of the future of the PRD, head to the LoK Ma Chau Loop. 想要一窥珠三⻆的前景，去落⻢洲河套区看看吧。

To catch a great glimpse of the history of China,head to Beijing and visit the Forbidden City, The Temple of Heaven and The Great Wall.

# 表示受欢迎

sth have a very wide appeal = sth is very popular

sth enjoys a growing appeal = sth is more and more popular

# 表达重要

Sth matters/counts = sth is important

Sth matters to sb 某物对某人很重要

# 表示重要的是 B 而不是 A

It is not A that matters/counts but B

It is not appearance that matters but the innor world of a person. 重要的不是外表，而是一个人的内心

# 对 A 来说几乎没什么比 B 更重要

Almost nothing matters more for A than B

Almost nothing matters more for a family than a harmonious family atmosphere. 对于一个家庭来说几乎没什么比和谐的家庭气氛更重要了。

# 表示堆满

be awash with sth 被某物堆满

The lake is awash with a variety of rubbish.
